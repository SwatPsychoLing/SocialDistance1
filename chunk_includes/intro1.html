Welcome. This experiment consists of one part, which should take 10-15 minutes.

For this part of the experiment, you will read a series of passages. Following each passage, you will be asked to judge how naturally certain pronouns refer to people or things.

Read the sentence. When you are done reading the sentence, hit the spacebar and a question will appear. The question will ask you to judge how naturally a given pronoun in the sentence refers to something mentioned in the sentence. When the question appears, you should click on a rating between “1” for not natural at all and “7” for completely natural.

Your honest judgments are important, so please take this seriously.
